About Me

Hello, I'm ARJUN PRATAP SINGH!

I am a dedicated and results-oriented Data Analyst with a strong foundation in Python, SQL, Power BI, and data visualization. At HCLTech, I specialize in transforming raw data into strategic insights that drive operational efficiency, performance optimization, and informed decision-making across cross-functional teams.

My journey into analytics began with a deep curiosity about how data shapes outcomes in business and technology. Over time, this evolved into a mission to unlock the full potential of data through analytical rigor, automation, and storytelling.

At HCLTech, I’ve led key initiatives such as performance monitoring, capacity planning, and root cause analysis using tools like Splunk, Excel, and Power BI. My work has resulted in a 15% improvement in server performance, 20% reduction in system downtime, and 90% success rate in certifying team members through targeted GCP exam readiness strategies.

I bring a collaborative mindset and a strong focus on quality, consistently resolving critical bottlenecks, streamlining operations, and delivering high-impact dashboards and reports that empower stakeholders. Whether it’s conducting trend analysis or mentoring junior analysts, I strive to deliver clarity, value, and actionable outcomes through every project.

My Philosophy: Effective analytics goes beyond just crunching numbers — it’s about delivering clarity, enabling better decisions, and driving measurable impact. I believe in clean, transparent analysis, data integrity, and continuous learning as pillars of success in any data-driven role.
